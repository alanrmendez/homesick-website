Cast & Crew

Alan Mendez - Writer | Director | Producer | Composer | Co-Editor | Julio

Alan Mendez is a New York City-based filmmaker. Specializing in the horror and psychological thriller genres, Alan has served as writer-director-producer for several projects, as well as written commissioned screenplays for other producers. Prior to his time behind the camera, Alan spent five years working as a stage and screen actor, having performed across the country from Phoenix, Arizona to the Public Theater in New York City. A multi-instrumentalist, he plays piano, guitar, upright bass, accordion, mandolin, and drums. Alan is the composer half of the musical theatre writing partnership Mendez & Aberman, and he has composed and produced original scores for numerous film projects. Follow Alan on Instagram: @alan.r.mendez, @mendezaberman.

Erin Bradford - Deirdre | Co-Producer

Erin Bradford is is an actor, writer and producer based in NYC. She is the creator and writer of the short pilot, Plant Moms (Austin Film Festival and Catalyst Content Festival official selections). TV/Film credits include: Marvelous Mrs. Maisel, Plant Moms, Prologue, and Dearest Stranger.
